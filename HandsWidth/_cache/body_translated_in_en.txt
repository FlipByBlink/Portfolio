<h2 id="概要"> Overview</h2>
<p> Intuitively measures distances using both hands!</p>
<p> Displays the distance between the tips of the index fingers of both hands.</p>
<p> Unit Options</p>
<p> Centimeter (cm)</p>
<p> Meters (m)</p>
<p> Inches (in)</p>
<p> Feet (ft)</p>
<p> Yard (yd)</p>
<p> Extra Functions</p>
<p> Indirect tap on the pointer to fix the position</p>
