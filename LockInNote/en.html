LockInNote

A notebook application specialized in "widgets"!

Overview

This is a widget-specific note-taking app for iPhone/iPad/Mac/Apple Watch/Apple Vision Pro.

When you want to take a note, you can write a note from a widget and check the note on the widget at any time!

The memo note design on the widget can be flexibly customized to meet various purposes.

Multi-platform support

  • iPhone: Home screen, Lock screen, Standby
  • iPad: Home screen, Lock screen
  • Mac: Notification Center, Desktop
  • Apple Watch: Complication, Smart Stack
  • Apple Vision Pro: Widgets
