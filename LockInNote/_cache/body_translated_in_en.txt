<h2 id="概要"> Overview</h2>
<p> This is a widget-specific note-taking app for iPhone/iPad/Mac/Apple Watch/Apple Vision Pro.</p>
<p> When you want to take a note, you can write a note from a widget and check the note on the widget at any time!</p>
<p> The memo note design on the widget can be flexibly customized to meet various purposes.</p>
<h3 id="マルチプラットフォーム対応"> Multi-platform support</h3>
<ul>
<li> iPhone: Home screen, Lock screen, Standby</li>
<li> iPad: Home screen, Lock screen</li>
<li> Mac: Notification Center, Desktop</li>
<li> Apple Watch: Complication, Smart Stack</li>
<li> Apple Vision Pro: Widgets</li>
</ul>
