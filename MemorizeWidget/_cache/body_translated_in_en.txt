<h2 id="概要"> Overview</h2>
<p> Display your memorandum book on your home screen or lock screen using the widget function. App for iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and Mac.</p>
<ul>
<li> Two modes: "stand-alone mode" and "random mode.</li>
<li> In "Random Mode," the notes displayed change every 5 minutes</li>
<li> Memorandum data can be imported from text or files (csv, tsv, txt, etc.)</li>
<li> Linkage with OS built-in dictionary</li>
<li> Customizable search function</li>
<li> Synchronizes data between devices via iCloud</li>
</ul>
