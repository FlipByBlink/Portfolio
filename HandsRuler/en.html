HandsRuler

An intuitive measure app for measuring distances with both hands.

For Apple Vision Pro.

Overview

Intuitively measures distances using both hands!

Displays the distance between the tips of the index fingers of both hands.

Unit Options

  • Centimeter (cm)

  • Meters (m)

  • Inch(in)

  • feet(ft)

  • Yard (yd)

  • Feet and Inches

Extra functions

  • Indirect tap on the pointer to fix the position.

  • Multiple measurement function.

  • Log function.
