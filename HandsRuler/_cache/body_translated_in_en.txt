<h2 id="概要"> Overview</h2>
<p> Intuitively measures distances using both hands!</p>
<p> Displays the distance between the tips of the index fingers of both hands.</p>
<h3 id="単位オプション"> Unit Options</h3>
<ul>
<li><p> Centimeter (cm)</p></li>
<li><p> Meters (m)</p></li>
<li><p> Inch(in)</p></li>
<li><p> feet(ft)</p></li>
<li><p> Yard (yd)</p></li>
<li><p> Feet and Inches</p></li>
</ul>
<h3 id="おまけ機能"> Extra functions</h3>
<ul>
<li><p> Indirect tap on the pointer to fix the position.</p></li>
<li><p> Multiple measurement function.</p></li>
<li><p> Log function.</p></li>
</ul>
